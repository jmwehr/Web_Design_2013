About

The Decker Library is the campus Library of the Maryland Institute College of Art, located on the first floor and lower level of the Bunting Center. The Library contains almost 64,000 monographs and 325 current periodical subscriptions, and provides online access to more than 5,500 full-text journal titles, and hundreds of digital reference sources in art and numerous other subjects. The main focus of the Library is in the visual arts, while maintaining a broad, balanced collection in the humanities.

Policies & Guidelines

  • • All patrons must present either a current MICA ID, a current BALC member institution ID or Decker Library alumni card in order to borrow materials.
  • • Books are borrowed for a period of three weeks for students and staff, one semester for faculty.
  • • DVDs are borrowed for a period of two days for students, two weeks for faculty and staff.
  • • If a book that you need is on loan to another patron, you may ask the Circulation staff to place it on hold, and notify you when it is returned.
  • • Any book may be renewed if no other patron has placed a hold on it - see student guidelines for more information.
  • • Overdue charges are $0.25 a day per book, $1.00 a day per DVD.
  • • A book is considered lost if not returned within two months of the due date. The borrower is then charged the book's full replacement cost plus a $25 processing fee.
  • • After hours, books can be returned in the bookdrop located at the main entrance of the Library on the first floor of the Bunting Center.
  • • Library & MRC users can view a list of their checked-out items -- and students may renew items twice -- using the Library & MRC Catalog by clicking on "My Account".
  • • You can also view your Decker Library & MRC account using your mobile device! The freeBookMyne app allows you to view your checked-out items, holds and fines, and allows students to renew items. Available in iTunes.
